Welcomme To Clean City Portal

The Clean City Portal addresses a critical gap in urban waste management by introducing a centralised digital system that improves communication, coordination, and accountability. By enabling real-time reporting, structured information access, and stakeholder interaction, the platform enhances service efficiency and promotes cleaner, healthier urban environments.

Our Services

Enable residents to report waste-related issues in real time

Connect users with registered waste collection service providers

Support authorities with data for monitoring and decision-making

About Clean City Portal

Urban communities face persistent challenges in waste management, not due to the complete absence of collection systems, but because of inefficiencies in coordination, communication, and public engagement

Residents often lack reliable information about waste collection schedules and proper disposal methods. At the same time, local authorities and waste collection providers do not receive timely, structured reports about waste-related issues such as uncollected garbage or illegal dumping. This results in delayed responses, accumulation of waste, environmental pollution, and increased health risks.
